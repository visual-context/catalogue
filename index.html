📝 Visualization notes

Scanpaths are time-aligned: blue marks the start of the movement and red the end. Colors transition through the spectrum as time progresses.

Heatmaps are time-weighted, meaning earlier and later fixations contribute according to their duration; brighter areas indicate locations where gaze lingered longer over the course of the trial.
